## How I got into this?

I got into music early in my youth — reggae, rock, ska and patchanka covers with Eztli Cuica
and later with the [ATL Sound System](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=wmUvAeYu0SE).
There I learned to create original music, not just play it.

A couple of years later I started a new project with [Carlos Wise (DUB☆NIX)](https://www.youtube.com/@dubnix),
diving deep into a genre that captivated both of us: DUB.
I had to move to Jalisco and paused most collaborations to focus on my software engineering career —
but the sound never stopped.

In 2022 I came back with a new project of my own.
I played with [Lion Army Sound System](https://www.facebook.com/profile.php?id=100076157704803)
at the "Plantón 420" in front of the Senate Chamber in Mexico City,
at [Pulques La Venta](https://www.facebook.com/pages/Pulques%20La%20Venta/2117464271650371/) near our hometown,
and at venues in Guadalajara with [Shaka Dance Sound System](https://www.facebook.com/shakadancesoundsystem).

Since then I've been dedicating more time and resources to this.
Over 20 years building sound from the inside — no school, no shortcuts, no rush.

What I do is not a genre.
It's a state.

## What can I do for you?

I bring my music to whatever space you have.

* **DJ Set** — digital playback, no complications; CDs or USB sticks if needed
* **Live Mix** — laptop and controller, [performing in real time](https://www.bitwig.com/) with more movement
* **Digital Mix** — [digital mixer](https://zoomcorp.com/en/us/digital-mixer-multi-track-recorders/multi-track-recorders/r16/) (8 channels) with built-in FX, no laptop needed
* **Analog Mix** — [Cymatic LP-16](https://cymaticaudio.com/product/lp-16-live-player-16-track-backing-track-system/) (16 channels), external FX boxes, full analog signal chain — requires focus, won't disappoint

In all modes I can bring a Melodica, and vocals when the space calls for it.

Not playing back a track.
Making decisions in real time.
